# Spike a learnings log (tiny insights from failure)

A running log of small, atomic "here is a thing to do differently next time"
insights, the kind that today scatter into whatever topical doc happens to fit
(this session put two into `docs/environment/testing.md` and
`docs/github/mcp-server-routing.md`) or evaporate. Seeded small as
[`docs/LEARNINGS.md`](../../docs/LEARNINGS.md) with those two entries; this task
decides whether to keep it, and in what shape.

**Why it is its own axis.** It keys on an **insight** (a correction), where the
tracker keys on a **task** (intent) and the merge guide on a **PR** (delivery).
So it is not the two-logs-that-drift risk the conventions warn about: it is
orthogonal, not parallel. The drift guard is that entries stay an index (a
one-liner plus a `→` to the durable doc), never a copy of the doc.

**Open questions to settle in the spike:**

- **Format and home.** One file (`docs/LEARNINGS.md`), or per-entry? Provisional
  name; confirm or rename. Entry shape: is `symptom / corrected move / → doc`
  enough, or is a tag/category worth it for retrieval later.
- **Harvest cadence.** The natural hook is the wrap-up: when finishing a PR,
  append the session's insights. Manual, or projected.
- **Projection from PR bodies.** The idea that fits the existing machinery: an
  optional `Learned:` section in the guide-PR managed region, projected into the
  central log at merge the way `scripts/build-merge-guide.py` projects the guide
  region into `MERGE-GUIDE.md`. Decide whether to reuse that generator pattern
  or keep the log hand-appended.
- **Graduation rule.** When does a learning stay a log one-liner vs. graduate
  into a topical doc (testing.md, github/, environment/)? The seed treats
  substantial ones as graduated-with-a-pointer; confirm that rule.
- **Portability.** If it proves useful, does it join the to-go bag
  (`docs/PORTABLE.md`) and get a mention in `CONVENTIONS.md`, or stay
  web-tools-local. A projected-from-PR-body form would ride the surfacing course
  a repo already runs.

**Deliverable:** a short spike report answering the above, plus either a
formalized `LEARNINGS.md` + harvest step or a decision to drop it.

Where does a text repeat itself?

window.selfsim answers that exactly and fast: a suffix automaton finds repeated passages and counts occurrences in linear time, and a minimizer dot plot turns the whole repetition structure into a picture. Each snippet runs in its own isolated frame with the kit injected — edit it, press Run.

Sparkline · Chat Timeline 5-min slots · bank & drain

An imaginary chat dialog laid out on a timeline. Each response is one bar; its height is the response size. Time runs left‑to‑right in five‑minute slots — a slot with no response is a gap (no line).

The catch: two responses can land in the same five‑minute span. Instead of stacking or dropping one, we bank them — queue the overflow and drain one per slot into the slots that follow, so every response still gets its own bar, shown back‑to‑back until the queue empties. Banked bars are tinted so you can see where the backlog spilled forward.

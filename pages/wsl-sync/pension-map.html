Washington Pension RCW Map

The retirement systems of Washington State and the RCW chapters and sections that govern them: the crosswalk the pension-bill classifier uses, laid out as a reference tree. Cites link to the official RCW.

Mirrors PENSION_MAP in lib/kits/wsl-core.js (mehrlander/web-tools); official chapter titles from the RCW corpus snapshot. Self-contained: save this file and it keeps working offline (links need a connection).

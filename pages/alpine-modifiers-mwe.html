The directive callback gets { modifiers, expression }. Modifiers is just an array of strings parsed from the dot-separated attribute name; expression is the attribute value, evaluated against the surrounding Alpine scope on demand. Mapping from modifier strings to daisyUI classes is hand-coded inside each directive — no proxy, no magic.

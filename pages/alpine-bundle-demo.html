Alpine directives. Edit the markup, watch it bind.

The directives, stores, and magics alpine-bundle.js adds on top of Alpine. Each block injects the bundle into an isolated frame — it self-boots Alpine there — so every snippet is a fully live, editable Alpine page of its own.

Colophon

Driven by demo.mount(…) from vanilla-demo.js (itself framework-free). Each block injects alpine-bundle.js, which loads Alpine and registers its directives inside the frame. The bundle also ships $clip/$paste and a dropdown auto-close handler not shown here.

The framework-free counterpart is vanilla-bundle.
